MORE ABOUT ME

I'm currently in Year 1 in the Common ICT Programme under Singapore Polytechnic's School of Computing.

I come from Canberra Secondary School, and has been a student there from 2018 to 2021. The memories that I have made there were the most memorable ones that I made and the friendships that I made were unforgettable, even until today I still keep in contact with some of my secondary school friends.

After secondary school, I managed to enter Singapore Polytechnic through PFP (Polytechnic Foundation Programme). I decided to enter PFP as I didn't want to enter ITE, and wanted to have an achievement in my life. I felt happy that I manage to enter because I didn't want the hard work I spent on studying for my N-Levels to go to waste. Looking back, although some of the things I learnt wasn't beneficial for my diploma, the one year spent in PFP was worth it as I got to meet many new people and make new friends, as well as have a glimpse on how polytechnic life is like.
